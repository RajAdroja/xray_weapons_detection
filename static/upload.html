About YOLOv5

YOLOv5 is a family of object detection architectures and models pretrained on the COCO dataset. It represents Ultralytics' open-source research into future vision AI methods, incorporating lessons learned and best practices from thousands of hours of research and development.

Key Features

  • Fast and accurate object detection
  • Multiple model sizes for different performance requirements
  • Optimized for production environments
  • Supports multiple export formats (ONNX, TorchScript, etc.)
